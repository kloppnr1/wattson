# BRS-031 — Price / Charge Updates (Prissending)

BRS-031 covers the distribution of electricity price and charge information from DataHub to market participants. WattsOn receives three types of messages under this process.

## Message Types

### D18 — Charge Information (masterdata)

Carries metadata about a charge: name/description, price type, VAT flag, tax flag, pass-through flag, and validity period. WattsOn creates or updates a `Price` entity keyed on `ChargeId` + `OwnerGln`.

**Price categories** are auto-classified from message metadata:

| Category | Classification rule |
|----------|---------------------|
| `Elafgift` | `isTax = true` and `priceType = Tarif` |
| `NetAbonnement` | `priceType = Abonnement` |
| `Andet` | Fallback (TSO tariffs, system/transmission/balance) |

An explicit `category` field in the payload overrides auto-classification.

### D08 — Charge Prices (price series)

Carries the actual price amounts per time step (hour/day/month) for a given `ChargeId` + `OwnerGln` + date range. WattsOn upserts `PricePoint` records onto the matching `Price` entity.

### D17 — Price Link Update

Associates or dissociates a `Price` with a specific `MeteringPoint` (by GSRN) for a given validity period. WattsOn creates or updates a `PriceLink` record.

## DataHub Endpoints

| Environment | Base URL |
|-------------|----------|
| Preprod     | `https://preprod.b2b.datahub3.dk` |
| Production  | `https://b2b.datahub3.dk` |

## Domain Entities

- `Price` — charge masterdata (ChargeId, OwnerGln, PriceType, Resolution, etc.)
- `PricePoint` — individual price amounts per timestamp
- `PriceLink` — association between a `Price` and a `MeteringPoint`

## Notes

- BRS-031 and BRS-037 share the same inbox handler; messages are disambiguated by the `businessReason` field.
- Pure domain logic is in `Brs031Handler` (static, no persistence dependencies).
